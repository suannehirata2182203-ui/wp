How I Learned to Deliver Fragile Packages from Poland

Real experiences, mistakes, and solutions from shipping breakable items internationally

Last updated: March 2025

When Fragile Means Everything

Three years ago, I faced a challenge that seemed impossible: sending my grandmother's vintage crystal collection from Warsaw to my family in Germany. What started as a simple favor turned into a crash course in international fragile shipping that I never expected to need.

After multiple attempts, broken items, and countless hours researching, I've successfully delivered over fifty fragile packages across European borders. This site isn't about theory—it's about what actually works when you're standing at the post office with a box full of glassware, wondering if it will survive the journey.

I'm sharing everything I've learned: the packaging techniques that saved my shipments, the shipping services that actually handle fragile items properly, and the documentation nightmares I navigated. No fluff, just real experience from someone who's been there.
